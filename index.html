Triple Y Health and Wellness Services

Affordable private care for those who want better without the wait.

Founded by Dr. Linda Peter, Triple Y Health and Wellness is a black-owned private practice in Faerie Glen, Pretoria East. We provide cost-effective, high-quality healthcare services in a respectful, confidential environment, with a heart for clients who want better than public healthcare but can't afford mainstream private care.

From reproductive and chronic health support to transformational life coaching, we're building a safe space for healing, growth, and restored dignity.

Book Your Appointment - Fast, Simple, Personal

Dr. Peter is available for patient consultations every Tuesday and Thursday. Book your spot online to secure a time that fits your life. No queues, no stress, no confusion.

Meet Dr. Linda Peter

Founder of Triple Y Health and Wellness Service | Author of Surviving the Odds | Certified Transformational Coach | Influential Woman of the year 2025 | MSOSCI | Phd

Dr. Linda Peter is a healthcare professional, author, and coach whose own story of resilience fuels her compassionate approach to care. From overcoming poverty, teenage pregnancy, and emotional trauma, she turned her challenges into a mission to help others heal.

As the founder of Triple Y Health and Wellness, Dr. Peter blends medical expertise with lived empathy. Her vision is to create a safe, dignified space for those who often feel unseen in traditional healthcare.
